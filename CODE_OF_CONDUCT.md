# 📜 CartaOS Code of Conduct

This project follows the **Contributor Covenant** v2.1.

---

## 🌱 Our Pledge
We are committed to creating a welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone.

---

## 🤝 Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Provide feedback with kindness.
- Assume good intentions.

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment of any kind.
- Offensive comments related to gender, race, religion, etc.
- Trolling or derogatory remarks.

---

## 📩 Reporting
If you witness or experience misconduct, please email:  
📧 cartaos-maintainers@protonmail.com
